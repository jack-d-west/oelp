HandsOnData: Privacy-Aware Learning

What We Did

Welcome to HandsOnData! Here, you can explore the techniques and tools implemented in this project to learn data analytics, privacy-aware models, and Linear Regression.

Features:

  • Applied two different types of noises:
    • Laplace
    • Gaussian
  • Enabled Clipping
  • Applied Linear Regression
  • Visualized different types of noises

Key Formulas:

  • Linear Regression Coefficients: \[ \hat{\beta}_1 = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(x_i - \bar{x})(y_i - \bar{y})}{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(x_i - \bar{x})^2}, \quad \hat{\beta}_0 = \bar{y} - \hat{\beta}_1 \bar{x} \]
  • Laplace Noise: \[ \text{Laplace}(b) = \frac{1}{2b} e^{-\frac{|x|}{b}} \] where \( b \) is the scale parameter.
  • Gaussian Noise: \[ \text{Gaussian}(x; \mu, \sigma^2) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi\sigma^2}} e^{-\frac{(x - \mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}} \] where \( \mu \) is the mean and \( \sigma^2 \) is the variance.
